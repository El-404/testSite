Attachments

Attachments references a social bond between individuals and institutions, ideas, values, or other individuals.
Generally, someone is less likely to commit a crime if they have a strong attachment to components of society.
This idea works because people would rather not suffer the punishments from those attachments. A common example of this is losing respect from a teacher, parent, or friends from commiting a crime.

This can go the other way as well. If you are someone who has a lot of criminal friends, you are more likely to commit a crime. Gangs, cults, drug users, etc.
