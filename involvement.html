Involvement

Opportunity costs associated with how a person spends their time, things like; Homework, chores, etc.
Someone who is more heavily focused on these things will have less time to commit crimes.

Social networks also help prevent crime. If you have a small social network, then you will feel detached from society.
You likely won't hold similar values and will be more likely to commit a crime.
This isn't a guarantee, it's just saying that social networks help prevent crime.
